About me

I’m Justine Inocando, a candidate for graduation with a bachelor’s degree in Philosophy at the University of the Philippines Los Baños. I have experience in layout design, photo manipulation, video editing, and video production, and I’ve been involved in event production since 2017. I am also a founding General Partner of Magis Productions Co.

Between projects, I actively write philosophy papers for publication and conference presentation. When I’m not working or writing, you’ll probably find me funding my addiction to buying books and trying out new restaurants with my friends.

My Skills

Project Coordination/ Management, Layout Design, Photo and Video Editing, Live Stream Production, Proficiency in the following software programs: Microsoft Office, OBS, Canva, CapCut, Filmora, Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro, InDesign, and Lightroom.
